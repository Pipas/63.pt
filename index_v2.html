How to play

The cards stay the same.
The rules get harder.

Teams take turns getting their teammates to guess cards. The deck stays the same all game — what changes each round is how you're allowed to give clues.

01
Forbidden words

Say (almost) anything

You can't use the words from the card's title, but everything else is fair game.

02
One word

Only one word.

No gestures. No sounds. No title words. You get exactly one word to nail it.

03
Charades

No words at all.

Gestures and sounds only. By now you know every card, so good luck miming it.
