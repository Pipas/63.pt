How to play

The cards stay the same.
The rules get harder.

Teams take turns getting their teammates to guess cards but with each round the rules change.

Round1

Say (almost) anything.

You can't use the words from the card's title, but everything else is fair game.

For “Home Alone”
"Christmas movie about being left solo in a house… with Macaulay Culkin!"
Round2

Only one word.

No gestures, no sounds, no title words. You get exactly one word to nail it.

For “Home Alone”
"Kevin!"
Round3

No words at all.

Gestures and sounds only. By now you know every card — good luck miming it.
